How will decisions be made? By leader, consensus?
Decisions will be made by consensus. In the event where there are ties, the leads of each subteam will be the main decision maker. I.e. if disagreeing on the structure of network architecture, the network lead will be making the ultimate decision. 
How will you communicate and collaborate online?
We have created a discord server as the center of our communication, hosting channels for brainstorming, storing important websites, art references, and subchannels for different aspects of the project. 
How will you know when you're off schedule, and how will you deal with schedule slips?
Outside of lectures, we have weekly full-team meetings every Tuesday, 2PM. We will roughly follow an AGILE SCRUM framework, described below:
At the beginning of the meeting, each team member will talk about their progress since the last meeting. Then they can discuss challenges they faced, and ask teammates for help and opinions. 
The team will come together and discuss the next step and assigning new tasks. 
Each subteam should also dedicate a set amount of communication time/meeting outside of the main meeting for their respective topics.
